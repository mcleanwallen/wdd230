Target Audience Description

This website is a simple one, and as such it is a place where users can come to get quick information without a billion ads popping up.
The target audience would be the person who only wants to see the weather for the local area(s) and not get bombarded by a ton of ads.

Personas

  1. A retired person who lives in the area.
  2. A student from 1st grade to 12th grade.

Scenarios

  • Retired person
    1. Likes to check the weather daily for his area and the surrounding areas (hobby)
    2. Check to see if it would be a good day to go fishing
    3. Check to see if the wind will be too much for his R/C airplane
  • School age kid
    1. Check the weather to see what he/she should wear to school
    2. Check if there will be a snow-day cancelation for school
